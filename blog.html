What Is Yoga Really About? (And Why It’s For Everyone)
By Solange Sarria • Yoga & Mindfulness

Yoga is not a pose. It’s a path.
In Sanskrit, the word “yoga” means “to yoke” — to unite. It refers to the intentional practice of bringing body, breath, and consciousness into alignment. It’s not about flexibility or performance. It’s about presence.
At Sodada, we teach yoga as a tool for deep restoration and personal transformation. Whether you’re recovering from an injury, managing stress, or seeking spiritual grounding — yoga meets you where you are.

Physical Benefits:
  • Flexibility + strength without burnout
  • Improved posture, balance, and core function
  • Natural pain relief and joint support
Emotional + Mental Benefits:
  • Reduces anxiety and stress
  • Improves sleep and emotional regulation
  • Builds confidence and self-awareness
Spiritual Benefits:
  • Fosters self-trust and inner peace
  • Deepens mind-body connection
  • Encourages alignment with purpose

Yoga is for everybody — and every body. And at Sodada, it’s taught with integrity, personalization, and reverence.
